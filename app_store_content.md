## English (en-US)

### App Name

poly.
```

### Subtitle *(30 chars max)*

Authentic Polaroid Camera
```

### Promotional Text *(170 chars max)*
```
Turn every photo into an authentic polaroid. Film filters, colored packs, video & time lapse — all wrapped in a timeless frame.
```

### Description *(4 000 chars max)*
```
poly. transforms your iPhone camera into an instant film camera.

Shoot in Photo, Video or Time Lapse mode and watch your shot develop into a classic polaroid frame — complete with film grain, light leaks and a caption strip that's uniquely yours.

FILM FILTERS & PACKS
Choose from 5 authentic film emulations and 5 colorful Polaroid frame packs. Mix and match to create a look that's unmistakably you.

YOUR CAPTION STYLE
Write a short caption on the front strip and a longer note on the back. Pick from 6 typefaces and 4 weight options for a signature aesthetic.

VIDEO & TIME LAPSE
Record video polaroids or set an interval to shoot a time lapse. All frames combine into a single polaroid video ready to share.

LIBRARY
Browse your collection in 1-, 2- or 3-column grids. Filter, sort, multi-select and share or save in one tap.

MAP ON THE BACK
Each polaroid quietly records where it was taken. Flip it over to see a live map, or replace it with your own handwritten note.

ICLOUD SYNC
Keep every polaroid safe and available across all your devices with iCloud.

POLY. PREMIUM
Unlock all 5 film emulations, 5 frame packs, all fonts and watermark-free exports in a single purchase or flexible subscription.
```
